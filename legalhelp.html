For the People, By the People

This initiative aims to provide a directory of legal practitioners, but more importantly, to share community experiences and reviews to ensure everyone finds fair and equitable representation.

Free Legal Help Directory

A community-curated list of organizations offering free or low-cost legal assistance. We will share details on how to apply and focus on services that actively support ordinary citizens, especially fathers.

The Court System Explained

We will demystify the South African court system. This module will explain the different courts, what each one does, and how an ordinary person can navigate them, including guidance on filing documents and representing oneself.

Know Your Rights Library

An interactive, searchable library breaking down key legislation like The Children's Act and the Constitution into plain, understandable language. Every section will be paired with actionable advice and necessary forms, contributed by the community.
