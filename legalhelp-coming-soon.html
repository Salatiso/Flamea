What is LegalHelp?

Knowledge of the law should not be a privilege; it is a fundamental tool for survival and progress. For too long, accessing and understanding the laws that govern our lives has been difficult and expensive. **LegalHelp** is being built to change that.

It is a comprehensive, centralized, and user-friendly digital library of South African law. From the Constitution to the latest case law, from business regulations to customary law, LegalHelp will be the single source of truth, accessible to everyone, empowering you to know your rights and responsibilities.

How It Connects Our Ecosystem

LegalHelp will serve as the foundational legal engine for all Salatiso platforms, ensuring every tool is backed by accurate, up-to-date legal context:

Flamea

The tools you use to fight for your rights as a father will be directly linked to the specific sections of the Children's Act and relevant case law within LegalHelp.

SafetyFirst.help

All Occupational Health & Safety guidance will be backed by the full text of the OHS Act and its regulations, accessible instantly through LegalHelp.

Sazi.life

Understanding your culture and language also means understanding customary law. LegalHelp will provide the platform to codify and explore these traditions.

**LegalHelp** is more than a database. It's a commitment to ensuring that no South African is left in the dark about the laws that shape their destiny. Launching 2026.
