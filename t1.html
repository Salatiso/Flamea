A Letter to My Son, Nyana, and a Tribute to My Father

Nyana, this page is my gift to you—a sanctuary of truth, struggle, and unwavering love. As your father, I’ve fought tirelessly to be part of your life, battling systemic biases in South African family courts that too often favor mothers over fathers. Every challenge I face, every court case, every word I write, is so you never have to endure the same hardships. This is also a tribute to my father, who passed in 1993 when I was just 11. Without his example—his resilience as an inspector in Transkei, his dedication to family despite apartheid’s weight—I wouldn’t be the man I am today, strong enough to fight for you. Here, you’ll find the facts: court documents, family histories, and my research, all laid bare for you to review one day. I want you to know the truth, to understand the lengths I went to for you, and to make your own decisions with clarity.

With all my love,
Your Father, Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni
April 20, 2025
