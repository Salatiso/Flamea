Chapter 1: Interpretation, Objects, and Principles

In this Act, unless the context indicates otherwise— "abuse", in relation to a child, means any form of harm or ill-treatment of a child, including—

  1. the causing of any physical, emotional, psychological or sexual abuse;
  2. deliberately neglecting a child; or
  3. allowing a child to be physically, emotionally, psychologically or sexually abused or neglected;

"adoption" means the legal process by which a person becomes the parent of a child who is not their biological child...
