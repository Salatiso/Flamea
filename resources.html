Resource Directory

A comprehensive directory of courts, advocacy groups, and support services for fathers and families in South Africa.

Courts
Constitutional Court

Role: Apex court for constitutional matters.

Address: 1 Hospital Street, Constitution Hill, Braamfontein, 2017

Phone: +27 11 359-7400

Email: director@concourt.org.za

High Courts
Handles serious family law cases and appeals. Each province has a division.
Magistrates' Courts (Children's Courts)
Primary courts for most family law matters. Every Magistrates' Court is also a Children's Court.
Family Advocate Offices

Provides free mediation for custody, access, and guardianship disputes.

NGOs & Advocacy Organisations
Organisations Supporting Fathers & Men
Cool Dads Foundation: Workshops, school drives. (Kwa-Thema, Springs)
Dads On Duty: Mentorship, life skills. (Ennerdale, Johannesburg)
Fathers 4 Justice SA: Advocacy, bonding guidance. (National)
Father A Nation (FAN): Restoring men as fathers and role models. (National)
Organisations Supporting Children & Families
FAMSA: Family and marriage counselling. (National)
Save the Children SA: Child protection and support. (National)
Sonke Gender Justice: Gender equality and engaging men. (National)
Centre for Child Law: Child rights litigation and policy. (Pretoria)
Legal & Social Services

Legal Aid South Africa: Free legal services for qualifying individuals. Toll-Free: 0800 110 110

ProBono.Org: Connects public with free legal services from private professionals.

Department of Social Development (DSD): Provincial offices provide social work services, family preservation programs, and child welfare support.
