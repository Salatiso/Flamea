The Story of FLAMEA

The Spark

A father-led advocacy movement born from the urgent need to reclaim our rightful place as equal partners in raising our children. We are fathers who refuse to accept a system that marginalizes us.

Our Mission

To fight fatherlessness, prevent crime, end gender-based violence, and restore morality by demanding equal parenting rights and challenging every discriminatory practice in our legal system.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every child grows up with an involved father figure, where young men choose purpose over crime, and where family courts operate without gender bias, upholding the Constitution.

Our Values

Safety, Assurance, Learning, Adaptability, Training, Integrity, Solutions, Operations. These principles guide every action we take.
