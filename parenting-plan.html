The Law is Your Foundation

Under the Children's Act 38 of 2005, the "best interests of the child" are paramount. A good parenting plan is not about winning or losing; it is a written agreement that proves both parents are committed to this principle. It is your roadmap to cooperative and effective co-parenting.
