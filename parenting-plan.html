The Law is Your Foundation

The Constitution's Bill of Rights is the supreme law. Section 9 guarantees equality, and Section 28 states the "best interests of the child" are paramount. A good parenting plan, as outlined in the Children's Act 38 of 2005, is your agreement to uphold these principles. It is the roadmap to cooperative and effective co-parenting.
