Risk Management for Fathers

Apply professional safety principles to protect your family's future.

Module 1: The Father as the Ultimate Risk Manager

As a father, you are the Chief Risk Officer of your family. The principles used to keep workers safe in a factory or on a construction site can be powerfully applied to safeguard your family's physical, emotional, and financial well-being. This course, inspired by the book *Safety First*, will teach you how.

A. What is Risk Management?

At its core, risk management is a simple, proactive process:

  1. Identify Hazards: What could possibly go wrong?
  2. Assess the Risk: How likely is it to go wrong, and how bad would it be if it did?
  3. Control the Risk: What can I do to stop it from happening, or lessen the impact?
  4. Review: Did my controls work? What can I improve?

    5. This systematic way of thinking allows you to move from being reactive (dealing with problems as they happen) to being proactive (preventing problems before they start).
